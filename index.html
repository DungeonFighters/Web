If you are a fan of exciting video games like us, you must have a special soft corner for hack ‘n slash games! And combining that with an incredible battle filled with non-stop enemies – what could be better? That’s exactly what Dungeon Fighters offers!

It provides you with everything that a video game lover wants. Slashing your way towards freedom through hordes of enemies with an oversized weapon in one hand – it just cannot get better than that! It stirs every nerve of a player with an adrenaline rush, the instinct to survive, and ultimately the triumph of destroying the enemies! It’s as good as it gets!

Created by MOCA GAMES, based in Rio De Janeiro, Brazil, the team behind Dungeon Fighters has a commitment in the making of fun and captivating games, delivering a great experience for players of all ages.

Fight waves of monsters

Face all sorts of unpredictable enemies, exceed your limits and keep yourself alive! All monsters have powerful weapons and are itching to use them against you! Flying spears, explosives, arrows, axes... – they have it all.
Be quick or be dead! Earn the maximum performance stars and keep going on unlocking new levels! Find clever ways around the enemies (and why not your friends?) to get the most kills and be the king of the hill!

Play with your friends

What could be better than playing an entertaining, fun, and exciting video game? Playing it with your friends!
Online games often have you play with strangers or bots. Well, that’s fun too, but nothing compares to the thrill of gathering friends in a room for a true game party!
This game offers single-player and local multiplayer features, such as up to 4-player co-op and versus modes!
